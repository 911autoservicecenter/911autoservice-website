What we work on

Our auto repair page covers who we are and how to book. This page is the longer list of systems and jobs we take on day to day — most cars and light trucks, maintenance through major repair. Back to auto repair & scheduling

Systems we service

If you do not see your concern listed, call anyway — we will tell you straight if it is something we handle or who to talk to next.

  • Brakes, steering & suspension

    Pads, rotors, calipers, brake lines, ABS concerns, wheel bearings, shocks and struts, ball joints, tie rods, rack-and-pinion, and steering or suspension parts that affect how your vehicle stops and tracks straight.

  • Engine performance & drivetrain

    Check-engine lights, drivability, fuel and ignition diagnosis, cooling system leaks and overheating, belts and pulleys, and engine work including replacement units when that is the right fix. We are a Jasper Certified Authorized service center for reman engines and related work.

  • Differentials, axles & driveline

    Driveshafts, U-joints, CV axles, wheel hubs, and differential service and repair. Our Yukon Gear & Axle certified master installers handle precision driveline and axle jobs.

  • Electrical, charging & starting

    Batteries, alternators, starters, wiring faults, lights, power accessories, and parasitic drain diagnosis.

  • Heating & air conditioning

    Blower motors, cabin comfort, refrigerant leaks, compressor and related HVAC repairs so you are comfortable year-round.

  • Exhaust

    Exhaust leaks, damaged pipes and mufflers, and related repairs that affect noise, emissions, and safety.

  • Maintenance & fluid services

    Oil changes, filters, transmission and differential fluids, coolant exchanges, and scheduled maintenance packages tailored to your vehicle.

  • Tires & wheels

    Mounting, balancing, rotations, pressure and wear checks, and tire-related repairs. Ask us about your vehicle when you call.

  • Light trucks, SUVs & work vehicles

    We service many pickups, vans, and fleet-style vehicles used for work — same standards as passenger cars, with an eye on uptime and safety.

Small engines, equipment, washers & dryers, powersports

Not everything with a motor fits the same schedule as a car job. Call with make and model first so we can confirm we can take it in and what to bring.

  • Outdoor power & small engines

    Lawn mowers (push and riding), string trimmers / weed eaters, chain saws, leaf blowers, hedge trimmers, pole saws, snowblowers, pressure washers, portable generators, tillers / cultivators, log splitters, chipper-shredders, trash pumps / transfer pumps, stump grinders (small / walk-behind), aerators, overseeders, sod cutters, concrete saws (walk-behind), vibratory plate compactors.

  • Washers & dryers

    We work on washing machines and clothes dryers. Call with brand and model before you haul it in. We do not service other home appliances (dishwashers, refrigerators, ovens, microwaves, window A/C, etc.).

  • Powersports & recreation

    Go karts, mini bikes, motorcycles, ATVs, UTVs / side-by-sides, dirt bikes, pocket bikes, mopeds / scooters, golf carts (gas and electric), snowmobiles, personal watercraft (jet skis), dune buggies / sand rails (trailered).

  • Trailers, farm & jobsite

    Trailers: repair and rewire, plus hubs, bearings, lights, brakes, and general utility-trailer work. Farm & property: compact / sub-compact tractors, PTO-driven implements (balers, brush hogs — often engine or hydraulics only, depending on size). Shop & jobsite: air compressors, gas-powered welders, light construction equipment. Bicycles & e-bikes: flat tires and basic mechanical (varies — ask first).
