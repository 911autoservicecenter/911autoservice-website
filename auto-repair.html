911 Auto Service Center is a full-service garage in Coldwater, Michigan. Our licensed mechanics combine hands-on experience with up-to-date training so your car or truck gets fixed right the first time.

Credentials & certifications

We invest in training and certification so you know who is working on your vehicle.

  • Licensed mechanics

    Our technicians meet Michigan’s requirements to perform professional automotive repair work.

  • ASE certified technicians

    National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (ASE) certification reflects tested skill in key automotive systems and ongoing commitment to quality service.

  • Yukon Gear & Axle certified master installers

    Our Yukon Master Installer certified techs are trained to Yukon Gear & Axle standards for driveline and axle-related work — expertise you can count on when precision matters.

  • Jasper Certified Authorized service center

    We are a Jasper Certified Authorized service center, so you can trust our work with Jasper Engines & Transmissions products and remanufactured components.
